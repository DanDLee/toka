 New Pages

To create a new page,

  • Add a link to it in this page (or on any other page where the link would be more appropriate):
    • Press the "Edit" button.
    • Go to the bottom of the page (or anywhere, really).
    • Type the page's name in square brackets, [Like This].
    • Press the "Done" button.
  • Click on the link.
  • On the Status Line, down at the bottom of the window, Notebook will ask if you want to create the page. Type "yes" or "y", and press Enter.
