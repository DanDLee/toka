WordClasses

An implementation strategy adopted from HelFORTH.

is

Compiling: compile a call to the quote. Interpreting: invoke the quote.

is-macro

Compiling: invoke the quote. Interpreting: do nothing.

is-super

Compiling: invoke the quote. Interpreting: invoke the quote.

is-data

Compiling: compile the value into the quote. Interpreting: leave the value on the stack.
