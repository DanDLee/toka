 Tutorial

This is a series of lessons on the Toka programming language. It's based directly on the tutorials for Forth and Open Firmware that were written by Mitch Bradley for the OLPC project.

The original lessons can be found at http://wiki.laptop.org/go/Forth_Lessons

I have made as few changes as possible. I truely hope this proves beneficial to all those who may wish to learn to use Toka.
