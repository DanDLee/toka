 BsortLibrary

This library provides an implementation of bubble sort for sorting arrays.

Provides:

  • bsort
    • array number_elements --

Example: 

 needs bsort

  10 cells is-array foo

   20   1 foo array.put
   50   2 foo array.put
  650   3 foo array.put
  120   4 foo array.put
  110   5 foo array.put
  101   6 foo array.put
 1321   7 foo array.put
 1310   8 foo array.put
  987   9 foo array.put
   10  10 foo array.put

 [ [ dup i swap array.get . ] +iterate drop cr ] is .array

 foo 10 .array
 foo 10 bsort
 foo 10 .array
